What is Aarohan Wissenova?

'Aarohan Wissenova' is an online competition which comprises a series of monthly contests, each contest being independent of the others. The winners of each monthly contest compete in another Round at the end, and the ultimate winners of the competition get a wild card entry into the Round 2 of Aarohan, conducted during our technical fest, APOGEE!


Why conduct an online competition?

Team Aarohan came up with this idea of conducting a series of online contests to be able to reach out to a larger number of enthusiastic students at their home. Through Aarohan Wissenova, we aim to provide all of them with a way to channelise their knowledge, thoughts and ideas in a productive manner. A lot of us are hooked to Instagram and Facebook when we enter our teens, so why not provide more avenues to be hooked to it for the right reasons?

Why ''Wissenova''?

Wissenova: / German /
ιννοωατιωε κνοωλεδγε

While Aarohan means Ascent in Sanskrit, Wissenova is German for Innovative Knowledge. Put the two together, and here is your way to ascent towards new, innovative knowledge, a way that fulfills your passion for deep understanding and testing of the concepts you've been learning all your school life!
Be sure to look out for some really cool content on recent scientific discoveries and developments, which will be based on a different monthly theme.
Detailed description about the conduction of Aarohan Wissenova:
1. The competition consists of a series of contests, each having a different theme and lasting for a month. Each contest is independent of the others and is hosted on our online platform partner 'Dare 2 Compete'
2. A passage based on the monthly theme is released every Saturday.
3. One question related to the passage is posted for class 9th and 10th and another for class 11th and 12th on the following Sunday.
4. Questions are posted on our Facebook and Instagram pages and the answers are to be submitted on Dare 2 Compete.
5. Answers to those questions are provided on Wednesday or Thursday for you to critique your understanding of the concept.
6. The winners of each independent contest are shortlisted for a competition that's held in February.

Who wins?

The series of online contests goes on till February, after which the top 2 students from each contest are selected to compete against each other in another round. The winners of this competition get invited to APOGEE! So do participate and secure your position in the Final Round to give yourself a chance to witness the technological extravaganza, APOGEE!
